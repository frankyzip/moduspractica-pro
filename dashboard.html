Welcome to ModusPractica Pro v1.1.9

Practice smarter, not longer.

An intelligent practice planner built on peer-reviewed motor learning science. Schedule what to practise and when, track your progress over time, and keep your scores and resources one click away — designed with pianists in mind, but equally powerful for any discipline that requires repeated, structured motor skill development.

What makes it different
🧠 FSRS spaced repetition engine — schedules each section at the exact moment your memory needs it
🎵 Automatic BPM progression — tempo advances proportionally as your motor memory consolidates
🛡️ FrustrationGuard — stops sessions before frustration encodes errors into muscle memory
📅 Schedule month view — calendar overview of practice days and upcoming due sections
🔗 Links on pieces — attach sheet music, videos, or files; open them from your repertoire in one click
📊 Statistics & streaks — practice time, progress charts, and optional Groq AI analysis of your history
🎸🩰🥋🎭🗣️⚽ Works for piano, guitar, violin, dance, martial arts, acting, sports — any skill built through repetition.
🔒 All data stays on your device. No account, no cloud, no ads.
